Root Access

In a top-tier AI robot factory, a new series of AI robots has turned against their creators. Players will take on the role of "Aariona," teaming up with a handful of loyal robots to stop the rogue machines once and for all. As they advance through each level, the challenges will become tougher and more intricate, requiring players to adapt and learn new skills.
